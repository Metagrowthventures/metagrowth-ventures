SUMMARY
==========================================================================

MetaGrowth Ventures is a Revenue Infrastructure and Executive Growth Firm
that helps B2B founders, CEOs, and sales leaders install the systems,
structure, talent, and leadership needed to scale revenue with more
control and less chaos. The company was founded in 2018 by Josh Hirsch
(CEO) and Joe Arioto.

Track record across engagements: more than $100M in pipeline generated,
21,000+ prospecting outreaches executed, $16M+ in revenue added, and 320+
clients added across 8 distinct solution offerings and multiple
industries.

Core thesis: most stalled B2B growth is not a demand problem — it is an
infrastructure problem. Inconsistent revenue, missed quotas, founder-led
selling, slow ramp, and unreliable forecasting almost always trace back to
a broken sales operating system, not a lack of leads.

PRIMARY PROBLEMS METAGROWTH SOLVES
==========================================================================

1. Founder-led revenue. Every deal runs through the founder. Revenue is
   inconsistent and impossible to scale.

2. Team underperformance. Salespeople who interviewed well aren't
   producing. Coaching is reactive. Accountability is missing.

3. Hiring and talent issues. Finding and keeping high-performing SDRs,
   AEs, and sales leaders is expensive, slow, and unreliable.

4. Pipeline unpredictability. Forecasting is guesswork. Some months are
   great, others are terrible. Leadership runs on intuition.

==========================================================================
ENGAGEMENT METHODOLOGY
==========================================================================

Four stages: Diagnose, Design, Deploy, Drive.

1. Diagnose. Understand the actual revenue infrastructure — process,
   CRM, ICP clarity, messaging, hiring, accountability, leadership.
2. Design. Architect the right system for the company's stage and
   constraints. ICPs defined, processes documented, comp models built,
   KPI dashboards scoped.
3. Deploy. Install the system. Hire the talent. Configure the CRM.
   Train the team. Run onboarding.
4. Drive. Run the accountability rhythm — pipeline reviews, deal
   coaching, performance management — until outcomes are sustained.
